Assisted Assignments

A New Learning Management System for Teachers and Students.

Catching Up With Modern Educational Needs

Assisted Assignments is a Learning Management System for teachers and students that introduces a solution for both under-complex assignment structures, and poor usage of artificial intelligence in learning environments.

Basic Learning Management System Functionality

This tool preserves normal functionality available in other systems such as Canvas.

Create Assignments With Checkpoints

We seek to solve the issues with disorganized and simple assignment structures that current Learning Management Systems fail to provide.

Start an assignment object much like any other system.

Add additional sub-tasks to organize assignment structure.

With our tool, you can break down assignments into sub-tasks that students can recognize as checkpoints for a greater project.

As a result, students have a clearer and organized way to interact with assignment tasks.

Artificial Intelligence That Talks To You

As artificial intelligence continues to develop and integrate into our society, we seek to find ways to productively implement this technology into the learning process.

Our artificial intelligence cognitive bots speak directly to instructors about student progress, and helps students with their homework alongside them in a guided format.

Task-Delineation

Task-delineation breaks larger projects into smaller, well-defined tasks. This ensures clearer responsibilities and more manageable deadlines.

Specific Outcomes from Task-Delineation

  • Instructors can sequence tasks in a hierarchy for clarity.
  • Students receive detailed steps with descriptions, goals, and instructions.

AI Assisted Assignments

AI chatbots help students by answering questions, simplifying complex topics, and providing personalized feedback.

Specific Outcomes from AI-Integration

  • Instructors view summarized student progress.
  • Students receive context-sensitive, task-based AI support.
  • Guided assistance is available for complex assignments.
